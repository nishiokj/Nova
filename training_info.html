Overview

This training program is designed to bring professionals up to speed with industry best practices through hands-on projects, weekly live sessions, and mentor feedback. By the end of the program you'll have a portfolio-ready capstone project and practical skills employers seek.

  • Format: 8-week blended learning (weekly live workshops + on-demand materials)
  • Outcomes: portfolio project, certificate of completion, career prep
  • Support: mentors, peer groups, weekly office hours

Curriculum & Learning Objectives

The curriculum focuses on practical skills with measurable objectives for each module. Instructors provide short lectures, guided exercises, and code reviews.

Module 1 — Foundations

Understand the core concepts and tools. Objectives: environment setup, version control, reproducible workflows.

Module 2 — Applied Techniques

Learn real-world patterns and project structuring. Objectives: testing, debugging, documentation.

Module 3 — Capstone

Plan, build, and present a capstone project. Objectives: delivery, presentation, and reflection.

FAQs

Who is this course for?

Professionals with basic familiarity in the subject area seeking practical, applied skills. No advanced prior knowledge required.

What is the time commitment?

Expect 6–10 hours per week including live sessions, exercises, and project work.

Is there a certificate?

Yes — participants who complete the capstone project and pass the final review receive a certificate of completion.

Capstone & Assessment

The capstone demonstrates applied knowledge. Assessments are project-based: instructors and peers review deliverables against a rubric emphasizing correctness, design, and presentation.
